Open Bay Caravan Park

Experience adventure at Open Bays Caravan Park, home of beautiful lakes, peaceful forestry, and family friendly camping grounds. Located within Port Arlington, its perfect for those who want to get back to nature, without being far from a bustling seaside town for those who need their morning coffee.

Our caravan park comes with many different types of accomodation, you can choose between small and medium
camping grounds, powered and unpowered, or book out one of our caravan sites.
